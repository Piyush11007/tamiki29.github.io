Travelnote

Travelnote is a collection of impressions of cities where I pass through. In this photographic series, I capture what catches my eye at first when I arrive in a new city. I like those first impressions because in a way, they are ephemeral. I can only see them with a fresh, spontanious eye. Once I get used to the new environment, they fade into the background, until they get invisible. To me, those first impressions always reveal something about a city's particular flavor.
